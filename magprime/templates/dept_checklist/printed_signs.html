Room Signage for {{ department.name }}

PLEASE READ BEFORE SUBMITTING!

Before requesting any signage, please review the current Signage Inventory to see if we already have your requested sign. (Use the ctrl+f find feature to search throughout the document.) You must use your MAGFest account to view the document.

All attendee-facing rooms will automatically receive either a 24"x36" coroplast or 33"x80" retractable sign for outside of the room. Please submit requests for additional signage ONLY here.

– DO NOT REQUEST A SIGN THAT IS ALREADY LISTED IN THE SIGNAGE INVENTORY DOCUMENT –

Signage comes in three types: 12"x18" coroplast, 24"x36" coroplast, and 33"x80" retractable floor banner. Be sure to specify FOR EACH SIGN:

  • Number needed
  • Size of sign
  • Primary text of sign
  • Sub text of sign (if applicable)
  • Intended placement location for sign
  • Link to any custom artwork that is needed
  • Any additional notes

The signage department reserves the right to reduce or decline requests according to their discretion. We will contact you if significant changes to your order are necessary. Please post in #super-signage for any questions, or any odd/complicated requests.

Note: This checklist item is NOT for printed materials such as sign-up forms, waivers, or anything else that goes on standard office paper. STOPS has a paper cutter and a printer for small jobs (less than 50 pages). Anything more than 50 pages needs to go into the Bulk Print Jobs checklist item.
