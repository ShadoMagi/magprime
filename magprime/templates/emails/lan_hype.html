As the year comes to an end, the MAGFest planning kicks into overdrive. This year in LAN, we have some exciting announcements to share with you!

Schedule

Our tournament schedule for this year is now available! While times are still being finalized, you can start planning for the action now. More details, including tournament rules, will be shared on our subreddit and added to the schedule as they become available.

PC Build Competition

Are you ready to show off your PC building skills? Or do you simply want to marvel at some cool creations? This year, we’re excited to announce the return of the PC Build Competition! Builds will be judged in categories such as technical achievement, artistic expression, and attendee choice. Take a leisurely stroll through the LAN room, enjoy the blinkin’ lights, and vote for your favorites!

PC Rentals

Can't bring your PC to LAN? Don’t worry—you can still join the BYOC (bring-your-own-computer)! Our friends at EmpoweredPC are offering PC, monitor, and peripheral rentals. Pick-up and drop-off will be on-site for your convenience. Supplies are limited, so make sure to reserve soon! Deadline is January 1st, 2025
