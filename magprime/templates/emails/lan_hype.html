Schedule

Our schedule for this year is now available. More information, including tournament rules and further details, will be coming soon on our subreddit as well as added to the previously linked schedule once they are available.

PC Build Competition

Are you ready to show off your PC building skills? Do you want to see a bunch of cool builds and vote for your favorites? This year we're excited to announce the PC Build Competition! Builds will be judged in the categories of technical achievement, artistic expression, and attendee choice. Attendees will be encouraged to have a leisurely stroll through the LAN, enjoy the blinkin' lights, and vote for the builds they like the best.

PC Rentals

Can't bring your PC to LAN? Don't want to wait for a public machine? You can still join the BYOC (bring-your-own-computer)!

This year we're excited to announce our friends at Empowered PC are offering PC, monitor, and peripheral rentals. Pick-up and drop-off will be on-site for ultimate convenience. Supplies will be limited so be sure to reserve soon.
