Reminders

We highly recommend bringing your own computer to fully enjoy the MAGFest LAN experience. Please note: Due to limited availability, we do not allow tournament participation using a public play PC.

Please pre-register your LAN items by filling out our online form to zip through initial check-in.

Bring an ethernet cable 20 feet long or longer. If gaming on a laptop make sure it has a wired network port or USB network adapter. Limited quantities of ethernet cables and network adapter will be available for purchase.

Please update your OS and games before coming to MAGFest LAN. We try our best to provide enough bandwidth for everyone—saving every little bit counts. We don't want anyone stuck waiting hours for updates while friends play!

MAGFest LAN Rules

  • Please try to keep your setup contained. You must be able to fit in a 30" deep by 36" wide space (half a table) if we reach capacity.
  • Use headphones, not speakers.
  • Leave networking equipment at home. We have plenty of ports.
  • Bring a power strip. You must only plug your power strip into the one we provide your table. DO NOT DAISY-CHAIN MULTIPLE POWERSTRIPS.
  • Enter and exit only through the checkpoint. Other doors are alarmed.
  • Sleep in your hotel room. Keyboard napping OK; sleeping bags NOT OK.
  • Please adhere to the MAGFest Code of Conduct. This includes the prohibition of smoking, vaping, and the consumption of alcohol in MAGFest function spaces.
